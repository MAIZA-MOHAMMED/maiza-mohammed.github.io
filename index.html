About

Mohammed MAIZA received his M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science from the University of Sciences and Technology of Oran, Oran, Algeria. He is currently an Associate Professor in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Oran 1 Ahmed Ben Bella, Oran, Algeria, and a research scientist at the Laboratory of Computer Science and Information Technology of Oran (LITIO). His research focuses on bioinformatics, computational biology, and machine learning, with an emphasis on high-dimensional gene selection, microarray data analysis, and hybrid evolutionary algorithms for cancer classification. He also works on robotics, computer vision, and autonomous vehicles.
